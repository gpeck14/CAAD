About us!

California Academy of Art and Design is a college located in Valencia, California. We established in 1957. We focus on design, marketing, merchandising, studio arts, and theater. It is located in California and is a top-notch school for the best students with promising interests in the artistic world. From designing to marketing, this school has proven to lead to success for its students in the art industry.
