Mr. Lauren's Fashion and Design

Welcome to Fashion and Design! I have been in the fashion industry since 1967 and have created a very successful and well-known brand. I recently stepped down as chief officer and am now Executive Chairman and Chief Creative Officer. While being in charge is fun, I enjoy teaching and have been doing it for 20 years now. My goal is to teach young people how to create successful and unique clothes, styles, and brands and market them.

Fashion Design is a class for students looking for a career in designing and marketing fashion. This particular major is generally broad and focuses on sketching, sewing, constructing patterns, and learning through technology how to market products. In this course we will work on color schemes, fabrics, styles, and shapes that are original and inventive. Students will discover the skills needed to conceptualize, present and develop finished garments from their original designs. We also provide a thorough understanding of the industry from historical and present-day perspectives.
