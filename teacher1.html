Welcome to Mr. da Vinci's writing class! I have been teaching Professional Writing for 20 years now and I am excited to work with you. Professional writing is a career-oriented writing program, and is one of the best majors for aspiring free lance writers. Within this major, students can choose from three different specializations: digital/technical writing, writing for non-profits, or editing and publishing.

From writing to reading to interviewing, students will enhance their journalism skills and strive for success. The curriculum includes writing, editing, and interviewing, then displaying the acquired information into a marketable story. Journalsim is especially important in today's world as there are so many events happening every day. The journalism major will help students find out information through asking questions and exploring the world for themselves.
